Marcus Pfister

  • Born on July 30th 1960, in Bern, Switzerland
  • His tertiary education started with the Art School of Bern, and later on a Graphic Design apprenticeship
  • He worked as a graphic designer before becoming a childrens' author/illustrator
  • He published his first book "The sleepy owl" in 1986, but it was his 1992 book "The Rainbow Fish" which caught international attention, and convinced him to work full-time as a childrens' author/illustrator
  • Since then Marcus Pfister has published 49 books, which have been translated into over 50 languages. Worldwide there are over 30 million published copies of his books
  • Marcus Pfister uses different artistic techniques for each of his books, the most well known is his holographic foil for "The Rainbow Fish"
  • Nowadays, Pfister lives with his family in his birthplace of Bern, Switzerland.
  • His hobbies include playing basletball and photographing wildlife
